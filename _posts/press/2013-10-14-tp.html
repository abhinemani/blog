"It’s this willingness to experiment on a small scale and evaluate the results that’s crucial to changing the current model of governing, Nemani says. 'One thing about our model is that, unlike traditional vendors who have a very specific app that they’re going to build, our model focuses on emergent outcomes,' Nemani says.
